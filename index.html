Our Story

Built on Integrity,
Driven by Vision

Rainbow Constructions was founded by Shri. Ratan Kumar Dey, a visionary from the heart of Kolkata, with a singular mission: to make quality homes accessible to every Kolkata family without compromising on construction standards.

Over 15 years, we've grown from a small builder to one of Kolkata's most trusted real estate names — driven by word-of-mouth recommendations from our thousands of satisfied homeowners. Our projects span North, South and Central Kolkata, with each development carefully planned to blend lifestyle, connectivity and comfort.

We are deeply rooted in the ethos of Kolkata — a city that values tradition, warmth, and community. Every Rainbow home reflects these values.

How It Works

Your Journey to a Rainbow Home

From first enquiry to key handover — a simple, transparent, stress-free process designed around you.

01

Initial Enquiry

Reach out to us via phone, email or by visiting our office. Share your requirements and budget preferences.

02

Site Visit

We arrange a convenient site visit so you can explore the location, view sample flats and get a real feel of the project.

03

Documentation

Our team guides you through every legal and financial document with complete clarity — no jargon, no confusion.

04

Construction Updates

Stay informed at every stage. We share regular updates on construction progress so you're never left wondering.

05

Key Handover

On the promised date, collect the keys to your new Rainbow home — ready for you to move in and make memories.
